HatchGood

HatchGood is a platform that allows designers to sell their furniture while allowing consumers to purchase well-designed products for less markup cost. We believe that everyone should have access to creating inviting and welcoming place. HatchGood makes designer goods more accessible.

Visit HatchGood App

Features

HatchGood has the following features:

  • Product listing and display
  • Voting of community generated products
  • Ranking of most viewed product
  • Custom upload of products to HatchGood

Technology

HatchGood was built on the following:

  • Front-End: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Gulp, & Angular.JS
  • Back-End: Node.JS & MongoDB
  • Hosting: Heroku
  • Version Control: GitHub
